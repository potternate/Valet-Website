The Highest Level of Customer Service in the Industry

Precision Parking offers valet services, directional parking, and shuttles for up to 15 passengers (larger shuttles available upon request). Valet Parking is available for special events (private party, wedding) as well as parking services for restaurants, hotels and medical facilities.

Our Staff

All parking attendants are well-mannered, well-groomed, courteous, and respectful. We maintain a strict hiring criteria with careful screening of applicants and a thorough review of references. Each Precision Parking valet attendant is enrolled in the Sacramento DMV Pull Notice Driving Program to promote driver safety. The program provides Precision Parking with continuous updates on driving records, allowing us to enforce our strict policy. Upon hiring, all employees go through a company training program to ensure safety and maintain proper customer service relations.

Doors are opened, guests are greeted with a smile, hands are offered to the ladies, and coat jackets are grabbed for the gentlemen.

Liability

Liability insurance with a limit of $2,000,000 per accident, included in the contract that prohibits the customer from liability in the event an accident claim is brought forth. Personal guarantee of owner, Derek Turner, to resolve any accident claims with the highest priority.

Parking attendants are based upon the number of guests attending an event with a minimum of two attendants so that someone remains “at the front” at all times.
